# Review Parent Prediction Protocol

TASK-038C preserves the four-branch TASK-038A lineage while constructing the
Review population from immutable TASK-037D and TASK-038B rules.

- `A2` has 83 initially executable parents and 13 structurally non-applicable
  records.
- `A3` has 83 initial identity parents and 13 repaired executable parents.
- The logical Review population is 179 branch parents backed by 96 unique
  executable rules.

The 83 initial-rule predictions are reused from the hash-frozen TASK-037E
inner artifacts. The 13 repaired rules execute twice in fresh rootless
containers on full inner values. Every prediction must be finite,
one-dimensional, length-matched, binary and exactly reproducible.

All 179 logical parent references are frozen before inner labels may be
loaded. Initial `A2` and `A3` parents may share a prediction hash, but they
remain separate branch transformations. Containers receive values only; labels,
detector predictions, metrics, outer data and sealed-test data are excluded.
