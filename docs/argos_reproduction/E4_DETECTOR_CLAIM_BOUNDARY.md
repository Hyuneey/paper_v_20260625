# E4 Detector Claim Boundary

TASK-037B may establish that both paper-aligned LSTMAD variants were fit and
evaluated under one frozen detector-only protocol across the ten existing KPI
series. It reports direct PA-free point/event metrics, AUROC, AUPRC and
descriptive alpha-minus-beta sensitivity.

It does not identify the exact ARGOS variant, select an alpha/beta winner,
reproduce the ARGOS benchmark, evaluate a sealed test, or establish detector
superiority. It does not test detector-rule complementarity because no fusion
arm executes. E5/E6 planning requires a separate approval after E4 completion.
