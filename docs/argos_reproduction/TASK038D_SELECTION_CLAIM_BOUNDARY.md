# TASK-038D Selection Claim Boundary

TASK-038D may report candidate availability, selected origins, no-op rates,
Repair and Review survival through inner selection, A0-relative selection
changes, and selection-split diagnostics.

It does not establish outer generalization, Review effectiveness, Repair
performance value, detector-rule superiority, full ARGOS validity, or sealed
test performance. Inner Full diagnostics are computed only after independent
FN and FP selections and cannot alter them.
